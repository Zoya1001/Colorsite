  Light-Color-based Palettes

  There’s really not much to say here, except for a few tips- all these
  palettes look good with white or an off-white shade to add a bit of
  neutrality, and they can be used in any way you want :)
  You can also dull down the color just a tiny bit if you find them too
  bright, and it should still look great. Have fun!
